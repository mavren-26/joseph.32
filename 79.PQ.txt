Problem 1 — Top K Frequent Elements
Problem statement

Given an integer array nums and an integer k, return the k most frequent elements in any order.

Examples

Input: nums = [1,1,1,2,2,3], k = 2 → Output: [1,2]

Input: nums = [1], k = 1 → Output: [1]

Constraints

1 <= nums.length

k is in range 1..number of unique elements

Aim for O(n log k) time (using a min-heap of size k) and O(n) extra space.
